Displaying the map in different projections using QGIS

In order to produce maps with different projections I had to change the Authority ID of the Coordinate Reference System, which is what I did for each of the following maps.

WGS84 Projection

As you move North and South the area becomes distorted, but remains relatively the same in the mid latitudes and longitudes.

Aitoff Projection

While the area distorts when you move North and South, it also enlargens when you move East and West.
