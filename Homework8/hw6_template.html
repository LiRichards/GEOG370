Homework 8: Geoprocessing

Lila Richardson

For my map I wanted to understand how pollution and poverty are connected in West Virginia, where my family is from. I used the buffer geoprocessing tool to visualize the pollution spread from powerplants around the state. I also used dissolve to join counties based on poverty levels from 25-30%, 15-25%, 10-15%, and 7-10%.
